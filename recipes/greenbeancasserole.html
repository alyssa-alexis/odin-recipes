Green Bean Casserole

Overview

This classic dish is usually served at Thanksgiving. But it can be had anytime. It is also a great complement to roast chicken or pork dishes.

Depending on baking time, the beans can have a crunchy or tender texture. Fried onions add a layer of crunch also. Flavor is mostly salty due to Cream of Mushroom soup.

Ingredients

  • Campbell's Cream of Mushroom Soup
  • Green beans
  • Soy sauce
  • Black pepper
